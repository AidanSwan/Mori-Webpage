About This Project

We are Team 05, also known as Raindrop Studios — a four-person development team from the Computer Science 426: Senior Project course at the University of Nevada, Reno, Spring 2026 semester.

Our project is Mōri: a first-person, round-based wave survival game set during Japan's Sengoku period. Over the course of this semester we've built everything from the ground up in Unity — combat systems, enemy AI, menus, sound, particle effects, and more. Visit the Mōri page to see the full game breakdown, or check the Leaderboard to see top scores.
